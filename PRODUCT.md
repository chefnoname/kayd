# Kayd

Hawala and remittance operators move money across borders every day — but most are still running their business on spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads, and manual end-of-day reconciliation. When you're managing multiple agents, regional offices, and currencies simultaneously, the margin for error is high and the time spent chasing numbers is significant.

Kayd is a purpose-built management platform for hawala and remittance businesses. It brings your daily rates, agent activity, deposits, office balances, and collection pickups into one place — so your whole team is always working from the same picture.

**Key features:**

- **Daily exchange rates** — Set the day's GBP/USD rate once and it's visible across the entire organisation instantly.
- **Agent & settlement tracking** — Log and reconcile settlements with individual agents, with a full history of what's been paid and what's outstanding.
- **Deposit logging** — Record incoming deposits against agents or accounts, track release status, and maintain a clean ledger.
- **Regional office balances** — Monitor the opening and closing balance of each office location, with end-of-day reconciliation built in.
- **Collection pickup coordination** — Schedule and track cash collection pickups across your network.
- **Role-based access** — Invite staff with the right level of access. Admins manage the business; staff handle their assigned tasks. No one sees more than they need to.
- **Audit trail** — Every action is logged. You always know what changed, when, and who made the change.

Less time reconciling. Fewer errors. Full visibility across your business — in one place.
